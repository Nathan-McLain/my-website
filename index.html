After this quarter, I am going into the Bachelors of Applied Sciences in Information Systems (BAS-IS) program. This class is one of the last prerequisites for the program that I need. After obtaining the BAS-IS, I would like to go into the networking or cybersecurity field as they are the most interesting to me at this time. Since I was young, I have always been interested in the mechanics behind how things work, and I believe that is what led me to where I am today and to be able to enjoy my hobbies. Taking Technical writing has helped me refine my skills in writing, look back at my academic and job history, and prepared me for future applications.
